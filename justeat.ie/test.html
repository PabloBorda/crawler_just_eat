Restaurant Info: Apache Rathmines

Restaurant Description:

Apache-Rathmines is centrally located in the heart of Dublin 6,Serving D.6,D.6w,D.4,D2, D8. Apache Pizza is your typical, tasty, American style pizza with all the usual favourites but where they differ from other pizzerias is they recognise the need to offer balanced nutritional food to the consumer. Apache are constantly trying to improve the traditional "deep fried" menu to give you the healthiest fast food possible. If you like to be different why not try their "wigwammer or "sitting bull" pizzas. Alternatively you can avail of a meal deal and include garlic bread, chips or onion rings in your meal. Don't forget you can always create your own Pizza.
